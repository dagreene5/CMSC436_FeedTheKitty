Description

The app allows a circle of people to fund an event. You can think of it as a hyperlocal version of Kickstarter, except it's used for funding small local events rather than startups or large events.
For example, suppose Alice wants to get together with friends to watch the super bowl. She would need various items such as snacks and drinks to host the event at her place.
She could open up FeedTheKitty to create a event, write descriptions of the event, set the date and time, and publish so everyone can see and feed the event with small donations.
The app integrates popular services such as Facebook for logins and Venmo for payment.
They could search any events using #hashtags and look at events they have created and participated.


FeedTheKitty is built by David Greene, Sang Lee, Dj DeBrakeleer, Sara Leroy, and Michael Zinsky as a project for CMSC 436: Programming Handheld Systems at University of Maryland.
